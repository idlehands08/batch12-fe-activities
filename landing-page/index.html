ABOUT US

Sichu Malatang was founded in the year 2019. The first branch was established in Double Dragon Plaza, Pasay City. Our mission is to be able to bring authentic Szechuan hot pot experience to Manila. To do achieve this, we brought in highly skilled malatang chefs all the way from Szechuan Province, China. We take pride in our high customer satisfaction and retention because of the rich unique flavors we are able to bring to the community.
