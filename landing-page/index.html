ABOUT US

Sichu Malatang was established in the year 2019. The first branch was opened in Double Dragon Plaza, Pasay City. Our mission is to be able to bring authentic Szechuan hot pot experience to Manila. To achieve this, we brought in highly skilled malatang chefs all the way from Szechuan Province, China. We take pride on our high customer satisfaction and retention rate mainly because of the rich, unique and authentic Szechuan flavors we are able to bring to the community.
