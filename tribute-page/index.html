Elon Musk

Who is Elon?

From spearheading the creation of reusable rockets, to creating a company that is pushing the limits of fully electric production vehicles, Elon Musk is a testament to what hard work, perseverance, and talent can achieve.

Some of Elon's Most Noteable Achievements

  1. Founded SpaceX, the company who developed the technology for reusable rockets.
  2. Founded Tesla, a company leading in production of fully electric vehicles.
  3. Acquired SolarCity, a company heavily focused on renewable energy thorugh Solar panel technology.
  4. Developed Starlink which is a satellite constellation which will be operated by SpaceX and aims to provide internet access to most parts of the Earth.
  5. Founded The Boring Company, an infrastructure and tunnel construction company that aims to dig tunnels efficiently to facilitate an underground transportation network.

Biography

Elon Musk, (born June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa), South African-born American entrepreneur who cofounded the electronic-payment firm PayPal and formed SpaceX, maker of launch vehicles and spacecraft. He was also one of the first significant investors in, as well as chief executive officer of, the electric car manufacturer Tesla.
