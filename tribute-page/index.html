From spear heading the creation of reusable rockets, to creating a company that is pushing the limits of fully electric production vehicles, Elon Musk is a testament to what hard work, perseverance, and talent can achieve. Although he can sometimes be highly controversial, people need to recognize what he has achieved and what he is constantly trying to do for humanity. He breaks barriers in order for human lives to become more sustainable through his inventions and his strong push for renewable energy. Apart from this, he is also pushing for humanity to become multi-planetary which is both useful and madly exciting.

I strongly believe that currently, he is the person contributing the most to propelling humanity towards a better future and that more people should strive to acquire his mindset.

"When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor." - Elon Musk
